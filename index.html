Conclusions & Next Steps
Conclusions
01
Data 360 is a complement of your data lakes and data warehouses — it acts on your critical data to unlock real-time decisioning without replacing existing investments.
02
Digital wallet, tierings, limit thresholds, and the application of best practices help avoid peak usage and keep credit consumption predictable and cost-efficient.
03
Start with a simple use case that brings clear, measurable value — early wins build internal confidence and establish the patterns for broader rollout.
04
A strong governance structure protects your data and facilitates implementation and maintenance — defining ownership, access policies, and data quality standards from day one.
Next Steps
1
Work with an implementation partner on the refinement of the initial use case — define scope, success metrics, and the data sources required to deliver value quickly.
2
Identify what telemetry data needs to be sent in a streaming fashion — map event sources, latency requirements, and the triggers that will power real-time actions.
